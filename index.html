The Gospel

I can confidently and proudly say I am Bible-believing Christian. I strive to love and serve God with my life and I also strive to love and serve others regardless of their beliefs, background, struggles. For this is the heart of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. What is the Gospel you may ask? It simply means "Good News"!

In order understand this good news we much understand the bad news. The bad news is that humanity is broken and corrupted with evil or sinful. This began when God first created Adam and Eve in the Garden of Eden. He told them not to eat of the fruit from the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil; but they were tempted by Satan in the form of a snake and the did the one thing God told them not. They disobeyed God and sin entered the world; along with death and much much suffering.

Those who are with sin are due their wages, for the wages of sin is death. However, it is not simply a physical death but also a spiritual one; eternal separation from God in hell. So the gospel is that Jesus Christ came down to Earth as fully God and fully man, he died for the sins of humanity (past, present, future) and then overcame death by rising again 3 days later! Those who believe in Jesus Christ and his finished work on the cross are gifted with eternal life and mending of the relationship with God that was broken in the time of the garden of Eden. We are not promised the next second, next minute, next hour, next day, next month, next year, and so on. So, I urge you consider this gospel and believe in Jesus Christ and his finished work on the cross.
